Construction CRM

Construction CRM

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology, a software development company, offers a Construction CRM designed to meet the specific needs of builders, contractors, and construction firms. This solution helps manage the entire project lifecycle, from tracking leads and handling client relationships to monitoring site progress and managing budgets. It allows construction companies to organize contacts, vendors, and subcontractors in one place while keeping records of proposals, contracts, and project documents The CRM also supports project scheduling, cost tracking, and daily reporting so that managers can easily monitor progress and spot delays or overruns. With mobile accessibility, field staff can upload site updates, photos, and reports in real time, ensuring smooth communication between the site and office. By combining customer management with project execution tools, Eagle Vision’s Construction CRM helps businesses improve efficiency, maintain compliance, and deliver projects on time and within budget.
