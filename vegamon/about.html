About Us

Nestled in the misty hills of Vagamon, Cloud Heaven was born from a simple dream: to create a sanctuary where travelers could reconnect with nature without sacrificing comfort and elegance. Founded in 2010 by the Thomas family, our resort has grown from a small retreat to a beloved destination for those seeking peace, beauty, and authentic experiences.

Our philosophy is rooted in sustainable hospitality and deep respect for the natural environment that surrounds us. We believe that true luxury lies in simplicity, connection, and the awe-inspiring beauty of the Western Ghats.

Every aspect of Cloud Heaven Vagamon has been thoughtfully designed to harmonize with the landscape while providing our guests with an unforgettable experience of Kerala's highland beauty.

Our Story

2010

The Dream Begins

The Thomas family purchases land in Vagamon with a vision to create a retreat that celebrates the natural beauty of the region.

2012

First Guests Welcome

After two years of careful construction using local materials and traditional techniques, Cloud Heaven opens its doors to its first guests.

2015

Sustainability Certification

We receive our first eco-certification for our sustainable practices and commitment to environmental stewardship.

2018

Expansion and Recognition

We expand our facilities while maintaining our intimate atmosphere, and receive the "Best Eco-Resort in Kerala" award.

2023

Present Day

Cloud Heaven continues to welcome guests from around the world, remaining true to our original vision while continuously improving our offerings.
