Point of sale

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology’s POS software is a comprehensive retail solution designed to streamline billing, inventory, and customer management while providing actionable business insights. It offers fast and accurate billing, real-time inventory tracking, and multi-store management, ensuring retailers can operate efficiently across all outlets. With offline functionality and cloud sync, businesses never miss a transaction, while advanced reporting and analytics help make data-driven decisions. Integrated with POS hardware such as barcode scanners, receipt printers, and card terminals, it delivers a smooth checkout experience. By combining speed, accuracy, compliance, and intelligent insights, Eagle Vision Technology POS empowers retailers to increase sales, reduce losses, and enhance customer satisfaction, making it a trusted solution for modern retail operations.

Inventory Management

Real-Time Stock Updates

Every sale, purchase, return, or stock transfer instantly updates your inventory. This ensures there are no mismatches between actual stock and system records.

Low-Stock & Expiry Alerts

The system automatically alerts you when items are running low or nearing expiry. This helps avoid stock-outs, wastage, and lost sales opportunities.

Batch & Serial Number Tracking

Track items by batch numbers, barcodes, or serial IDs—crucial for industries like pharmacy, electronics, or FMCG where traceability matters.

Multi-Location Inventory

If you run multiple outlets or warehouses, the system provides centralized visibility. You can view, compare, and manage stock across all stores from a single dashboard.

Reorder Management

Based on sales history and stock levels, the system suggests reorder quantities to ensure you always maintain the right stock balance.

Stock Transfers & Adjustments

Easily move products between outlets or warehouses and adjust stock for returns, damages, or shrinkage with proper audit trails.

Business Benefits

  • Save Time: Automated updates remove the need for manual stock checks.
  • Boost Sales: Popular products never go out of stock, ensuring maximum sales opportunities.
  • Cut Losses: Expiry and low-stock alerts prevent wastage and overstocking.
  • Better Insights: Reports show fast-moving vs. slow-moving products, seasonal demand, and supplier performance.
  • Compliance Ready: Full stock traceability for audits, GST, and industry regulations.

Offline & Cloud Sync

Offline Mode

  • When the internet connection drops, the POS automatically switches to offline mode.
  • Billing continues without disruption.
  • All transactions are stored securely in the local system/device.

Sales Analysis

  • Track daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly sales per store or outlet.
  • Analyze product-wise, category-wise, and brand-wise performance.
  • Identify peak hours and seasonal trends to optimize staffing and stock levels.

Cloud Sync

  • Once the internet is restored, the system automatically syncs all offline transactions to the cloud.
  • No manual work needed; the process is seamless.
  • Ensures that sales data, inventory, and reports remain up to date across all stores.

Secure Backups

  • Transactions are encrypted and stored safely.
  • Automatic cloud backups protect against data loss.

Benefits

  • Uninterrupted Billing – Continue sales even if the internet fails.
  • No Data Loss – Every transaction is saved locally and synced later.
  • Centralized Updates – Inventory, sales reports, and customer records are consistent across all outlets once synced.
  • Flexibility – Works for single stores as well as multi-location retail chains.
  • Peace of Mind – Retailers don’t have to worry about losing sales due to network issues.
