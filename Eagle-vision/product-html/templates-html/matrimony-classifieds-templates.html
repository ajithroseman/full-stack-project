Overview

Eagle Vision Technology offers feature-rich, user-friendly templates designed for matrimony and classifieds platforms. Whether you're building a matrimonial website for matchmaking or a marketplace for buying and selling, our templates are tailored to help you connect users, streamline processes, and ensure a smooth experience for everyone involved.

Ideal For

    Matrimony Platforms:

  • Marriage Websites & Matchmaking Agencies
  • Community or Religion-Based Matrimony Sites
  • Niche Matchmaking Services

    • Classifieds Platforms:

  • Buy & Sell Websites
  • Local Classified Ads Platforms
  • Job Listings & Services Marketplace
  • Event and Ticketing Websites

Key feature: Matrimony

User Registration & Profile Creation

  • Easy sign-up process for individuals looking to find matches
  • Detailed profile creation with fields for personal information, preferences, photos, and more
  • Privacy settings for each profile to manage visibility and sharing

Advanced Search & Matchmaking Algorithms

  • Filtered search by age, location, interests, religion, etc.
  • Compatibility algorithms to suggest potential matches based on shared preferences
  • Matchmaker features for admin to manually suggest profiles to users

Private Messaging & Communication Tools

  • Secure private messaging between registered users
  • Ability to share contact information, schedule video calls, or send virtual gifts
  • Interest notifications when a user views, likes, or messages another profile

Subscription & Premium Membership Options

  • Monetization options with free, paid, or subscription-based membership models
  • Premium features like enhanced visibility, advanced matchmaking, and profile boosts
  • Payment gateway integration for transactions

Photo & Video Uploads

  • Allow users to upload images and videos to their profiles
  • Moderation system to ensure profile content is appropriate and secure

Event & Wedding Planner Integration

  • Built-in wedding planner tools or integration with event management services
  • Calendar and task lists for upcoming events, appointments, and meetings

Privacy & Security Features

  • Strong encryption for user data
  • Ability to control who can see your profile, send messages, and connect with you
  • Report and block users for inappropriate content or behavior

Admin Dashboard

  • Manage user profiles, memberships, and content
  • Monitor activity, track messages, and handle disputes
  • Analytics to track user engagement, profile visits, and membership growth

Key feature: Classifieds

Product/Service Listings

  • Simple and easy-to-use interface for users to post ads with descriptions, prices, and images
  • Multiple categories (e.g., real estate, electronics, cars, jobs) for easy browsing
  • Option for users to post ads for both items for sale and services offered

Search & Filters

  • Powerful search functionality with filters for price, location, and category
  • Sorting options (e.g., by date, price, popularity) to help users find what they need quickly

User Reviews & Ratings

  • Allow users to rate and review products, services, or sellers
  • Build trust within the community and increase credibility for sellers

Messaging & Inquiry System

  • In-app messaging for users to contact sellers or buyers directly
  • Instant notifications for new messages, offers, or replies

Product Comparison Tool

  • Side-by-side comparison of products based on features, price, and ratings
  • Help users make informed buying decisions

Admin Control Panel

  • Easy management of all listings, payments, and user accounts
  • Options to approve or reject ads, manage dispute resolution, and moderate content
  • Revenue tracking for paid listings or premium features

Payment Gateway Integration

  • Support for secure transactions with payment methods like PayPal, Stripe, or credit/debit cards
  • Options for both free and paid ads, as well as paid subscriptions for sellers or buyers

Ad Expiry & Renewal System

  • Automatic expiration of ads after a specified time, with options to renew or edit listings
  • Notifications for users when their ad is about to expire

Optional Add-ons

  • Social media integration for easy sign-up via Facebook, Google, etc.
  • Referral program for rewarding users who bring in new members
  • Location-based services for geo-targeted ads or matchmaking
  • Multi-language & multi-currency options for global reach
  • Push notifications for new matches, messages, or deals
  • SMS & email alerts for important updates or promotions
