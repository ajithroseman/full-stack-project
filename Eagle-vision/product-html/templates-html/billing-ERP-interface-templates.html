Overview

Eagle Vision Technology provides comprehensive and customizable templates for Billing & ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Interfaces. Our templates are designed to help businesses integrate core functions such as billing, invoicing, inventory management, accounting, and customer relationship management into one cohesive platform, enabling smoother operations and better decision-making.

Ideal For

  • Small to Large Enterprises
  • Retail Businesses & E-Commerce Platforms
  • Manufacturers & Suppliers
  • Service Providers (IT, Consulting, etc.)
  • Financial Services & Accounting Firms
  • Inventory Management Systems
  • Project-Based Businesses (Construction, Consulting)
  • Subscription-Based Services

Key feature

Billing & Invoicing System

  • Automatic Invoicing: Automatically generate and send invoices based on completed transactions
  • Recurring Billing: Set up subscription-based or recurring billing schedules
  • Invoice Customization: Customize invoice templates with business branding, payment terms, and due dates
  • Multiple Payment Methods: Support for a range of payment gateways (credit card, PayPal, bank transfers)
  • Invoice History & Tracking: Easy access to historical invoices, payment status, and outstanding amounts

ERP Modules Integration

  • Accounting: Track financial transactions, balance sheets, profit and loss statements
  • Inventory Management: Monitor stock levels, track product sales, and generate purchase orders
  • Supply Chain Management: Integrate suppliers and distributors to streamline procurement and delivery
  • Human Resource Management: Manage payroll, employee records, leave, and attendance
  • Project Management: Track project progress, budgets, and resource allocation
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM): Centralize customer data, sales interactions, and communication logs

Order Management System (OMS)

  • Order Tracking: Manage orders from creation to delivery
  • Real-Time Updates: Automatically update order status and shipping details
  • Inventory Sync: Real-time sync between orders and stock levels to avoid overbooking or stockouts
  • Shipping & Delivery Integration: Integration with courier services for easy tracking and label generation

Customizable Dashboards & Reporting

  • Executive Dashboard: Real-time overview of financial performance, sales, expenses, and operational status
  • Detailed Reports: Generate detailed financial, sales, inventory, and project reports
  • Customizable Reports: Design custom reports to track KPIs, metrics, or other business-specific data
  • Data Visualization: Use graphs, pie charts, and tables for easy-to-understand data presentation

Data Security & Access Control

  • User Roles & Permissions: Control who can access what information based on user roles (e.g., admin, accountant, manager, employee)
  • Audit Trail: Maintain a log of all user actions, including invoice generation, order modifications, and payment processing
  • Data Encryption: Ensure sensitive business and customer data is encrypted for maximum security

Real-Time Synchronization

  • Cross-Module Sync: Ensure that all business operations (billing, inventory, HR) are connected and data flows seamlessly
  • Multi-Location Support: Synchronize information across multiple branches or store locations
  • Cloud Integration: Access and manage ERP data remotely via cloud platforms for real-time updates

Customer Portal & Self-Service

  • Client Dashboard: Allow customers to view their invoices, payment status, and order history
  • Payment Gateway Integration: Enable customers to make payments directly through the portal
  • Invoice Download: Customers can download or print invoices for their records
  • Support Tickets: Integrate a support ticketing system for customer service requests and issues

Integration with Third-Party Tools

  • Accounting Software Integration: Sync with tools like QuickBooks, Xero, or Zoho Books for accounting
  • Payment Processors: Connect with Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, or other payment gateways for seamless transactions
  • ERP System Sync: Integrate with other ERP systems or APIs for enhanced functionality (e.g., SAP, Oracle)
  • E-Commerce Integration: Sync with popular platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or Magento for seamless billing and order management

Optional Add-ons

  • Customizable Tax Calculation: Set up tax rates based on location, product type, or service
  • Multiple Currencies & Languages: Handle international transactions with currency conversion and multi-language support
  • Subscription Management: Manage customer subscriptions, renewals, and cancellations
  • Expense Tracking: Track and categorize business expenses, monitor cost-to-revenue ratios
  • Mobile App Support: Access ERP data and billing details via a mobile app for on-the-go updates
