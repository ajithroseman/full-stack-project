Nearby Places to Explore

Vagamon is nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, surrounded by stunning natural beauty. From rolling hills to spiritual sites, explore these incredible destinations just a short journey from our resort.

Vagamon Meadows

2 km from resort

Experience the breathtaking green meadows of Vagamon, often called the "Scotland of Asia". These rolling hills offer spectacular views, especially during sunrise and sunset when the mist creates a magical atmosphere.

  • Ideal for picnics and photography
  • Paragliding opportunities
  • Best visited between September and May
  • Easy to moderate hiking trails

Pine Forest

5 km from resort

Walk through the serene pine forests planted during British colonial rule. The tall pine trees create a beautiful canopy, with sunlight filtering through to create magical patterns on the forest floor.

  • Peaceful walking paths
  • Popular filming location for movies
  • Bird watching opportunities
  • Cool climate even during summer

Murugan Mala

8 km from resort

A spiritual hill featuring a beautiful statue of Lord Murugan, offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys. This peaceful site is especially significant during the annual festival of Thaipusam.

  • Spiritual and meditation spot
  • Spectacular 360-degree views
  • Colorful festival celebrations
  • Short hike to the summit

Barren Hills

6 km from resort

An unusual landscape of rolling hills without vegetation, offering a stark contrast to the lush green surroundings. The unique topography makes it a favorite spot for adventure enthusiasts and photographers.

  • Popular for paragliding
  • Stunning photography opportunities
  • Best visited during golden hour
  • Adventure activities available

Tea Estates

3 km from resort

Visit the sprawling tea plantations that blanket the hills of Vagamon. Learn about tea processing, enjoy fresh tea tasting sessions, and walk through the beautifully manicured tea gardens.

  • Tea factory tours available
  • Fresh tea tasting sessions
  • Beautiful photo opportunities
  • Learn about tea processing

Cloud Heaven Vagamon

Experience luxury amidst the clouds. A serene retreat in the heart of Vagamon's misty hills, where nature meets comfort.
