Restaurant Management

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology Restaurant Management Software is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline restaurant operations, enhance customer experience, and optimize business performance. It integrates order management, billing, inventory tracking, table reservations, and kitchen workflow into a single platform, enabling restaurant owners and managers to operate efficiently. With features like real-time reporting, sales analytics, and customer management, the software provides actionable insights to improve profitability and service quality. It also supports multi-outlet operations, online ordering, and integration with POS hardware such as receipt printers and card machines, ensuring smooth, fast, and accurate service. By automating routine tasks and centralizing management, Eagle Vision Technology Restaurant Management Software helps restaurants save time, reduce errors, increase revenue, and deliver an exceptional dining experience.

  • Order Taking: Quick entry via POS, mobile devices, or tablets.
  • Split Orders & Bills: Manage shared tables and split payments easily.

Menu Management

Multi-Outlet & Multi-Device Support

Analytics & Reporting

Online Ordering & Delivery Integration

  • Sync online and offline orders in one system.

Table & Order Management

Table Management

  • Visual Table Layouts: Display the restaurant floor plan digitally, including tables, booths, and sections for easy tracking.
  • Table Status: Monitor real-time status of tables – available, reserved, occupied, or in service.
  • Reservations & Walk-ins: Manage bookings and walk-in guests seamlessly; assign tables based on party size or special requests.
  • Waitlist Management: Automatically track waiting customers and notify staff when a table becomes available.
  • Multi-Zone Support: Manage multiple dining areas (indoor, outdoor, VIP sections) efficiently.

Order Management

  • Order Entry: Capture dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders quickly via POS terminals, tablets, or mobile devices.
  • Menu Customization: Add special requests, modifiers, or ingredient preferences per order.
  • Kitchen Integration: Orders are automatically sent to the kitchen display system or printers for preparation, minimizing errors.
  • Real-Time Order Tracking: Monitor order status from placement to delivery, ensuring timely service.
  • Split Orders & Billing: Handle shared tables, separate bills, and partial payments effortlessly.

Benefits of Table & Order Management

  • Faster Service: Reduce order-taking errors and improve table turnover.
  • Better Resource Utilization: Optimize seating arrangements and avoid overcrowding.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience: Smooth reservation handling and accurate orders increase satisfaction.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlined communication between waitstaff and kitchen minimizes delays.
  • Data Insights: Analyze table occupancy, peak hours, and popular menu items for smarter decision-making.

Inventory & Stock Management

Real-Time Stock Tracking

  • Monitor ingredient and item usage in real time.
  • Automatically deduct stock as items are sold or consumed in recipes.
  • Track stock levels across multiple storage locations (kitchen, bar, freezer, pantry).

Low-Stock Alerts & Reorder Management

  • Receive automatic notifications when stock falls below predefined levels.
  • Generate purchase orders or procurement requests directly from the system.
  • Helps prevent stockouts and ensures uninterrupted kitchen operations.

Batch & Expiry Management

  • Track batches of perishable ingredients with expiry dates.
  • First-expire, first-out (FEFO) management reduces food wastage.
  • Maintain compliance with food safety and health regulations.

Recipe-Based Stock Consumption

  • Deduct ingredient quantities based on menu recipes automatically.
  • Supports complex dishes with multiple ingredients.
  • Ensures accurate food cost calculations per dish and per menu item.

Supplier & Purchase Integration

  • Manage supplier details, pricing, and order history.
  • Compare vendor costs and lead times for better procurement decisions.
  • Generate purchase orders directly from low-stock alerts.

Inventory Reporting & Analytics

  • Detailed reports on stock usage, wastage, and item-wise consumption.
  • Analyze inventory turnover and forecast future requirements.
  • Supports data-driven decision-making for menu planning and cost optimization.

Benefits for Restaurants

  • Reduce Food Wastage: Track expiry and batch usage effectively.
  • Optimize Costs: Avoid overstocking or emergency purchases at higher rates.
  • Ensure Smooth Operations: Prevent stockouts for popular menu items.
  • Improve Profitability: Accurate ingredient tracking helps maintain proper food cost margins.
  • Compliance Ready: Maintain records for audits and food safety regulations.

Kitchen Display System (KDS) Integration

Real-Time Order Display

  • Orders placed by waitstaff, online platforms, or self-service kiosks appear instantly on the kitchen screens.
  • Prioritizes orders based on time, table, or preparation type.
  • Reduces reliance on printed tickets and manual handovers.

Order Management & Tracking

  • Track each order’s status: received, in preparation, ready, or served.
  • Allows kitchen staff to update the status in real time, visible to FOH and waitstaff.
  • Helps prevent missed or delayed orders.

Menu & Modifier Management

  • Supports multiple menu items, modifiers, and special instructions (e.g., no onions, extra spicy).
  • Changes to orders reflect instantly in the KDS, ensuring accurate preparation.

Multi-Kitchen Support

  • Supports multiple kitchen sections (e.g., appetizers, main course, desserts, beverages).
  • Orders are routed to the correct kitchen display based on item type, reducing confusion and improving workflow.

Performance & Analytics

  • Monitors kitchen efficiency: prep time per item, average order completion time, and peak-hour performance.
  • Provides insights to optimize staff allocation and menu workflow.

Hardware Integration

  • Compatible with touchscreens, monitors, and tablets in the kitchen.
  • Can be connected with printers for backup or for staff who prefer printed tickets.
  • Synchronizes with POS terminals, online orders, and mobile applications.

Business Benefits

  • Faster Order Processing: Orders flow instantly to the kitchen, reducing wait times.
  • Improved Accuracy: Minimizes human errors from handwritten tickets.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlines workflow across kitchen sections and staff.
  • Better Customer Experience: Quick, accurate orders lead to higher customer satisfaction.
  • Data Insights: Track kitchen performance, menu popularity, and order times to optimize operations.

Customer Management

Customer Profiling

  • Maintain detailed profiles including name, contact information, dining preferences, special occasions, and dietary restrictions.
  • Track visit frequency, order history, and average spending per visit.
  • Store customer feedback and special requests to provide personalized dining experiences.

Reservation & Guest Management

  • Manage table bookings, waitlists, and walk-ins efficiently.
  • Record customer preferences such as preferred tables or favorite dishes.
  • Ensure smooth dining experience for repeat and VIP customers.

Order History Tracking

  • View all past orders by each customer to analyze their preferences.
  • Recommend items based on prior visits or trending menu items.
  • Use historical data to enhance personalization and upselling opportunities.

Loyalty Programs

Points-Based Rewards

  • Reward customers with points for every order or spend.
  • Points can be redeemed for discounts, complimentary dishes, or special offers.
  • Encourage repeat visits and increased average bill value.

Tiered Loyalty Programs

  • Create multiple membership tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum) with escalating benefits.
  • Higher-tier customers receive exclusive offers, priority reservations, or birthday perks.
  • Incentivizes regular customers to engage more and move up tiers.

Promotions & Offers

  • Run targeted promotions for loyal customers based on dining history.
  • Send notifications about special menus, seasonal offers, or events.
  • Increase engagement and drive repeat business.

Analytics & Insights

  • Track customer behavior, loyalty program performance, and redemption rates.
  • Identify high-value customers and tailor marketing strategies accordingly.
  • Make data-driven decisions to optimize loyalty campaigns and retention.

Benefits for Restaurants

  • Increased Customer Retention: Personalized experiences and rewards encourage repeat visits.
  • Higher Revenue: Upselling and loyalty rewards drive average spend per customer.
  • Operational Efficiency: Streamlined customer and reservation management reduces errors.
  • Enhanced Engagement: Targeted promotions and loyalty incentives strengthen customer relationships.
  • Data-Driven Marketing: Insights from order history and loyalty programs guide marketing strategies.

Online Ordering & Delivery Integration

Online Ordering Integration

  • Multi-Channel Orders: Accept orders from your website, mobile app, and third-party delivery platforms (like Swiggy, Zomato, UberEats, etc.).
  • Real-Time Menu Updates: Update menu items, pricing, and availability instantly across all online channels.
  • Customizable Ordering Options: Allow customers to select add-ons, special instructions, meal customization, and portion sizes.
  • Automated Order Capture: Orders automatically appear in the POS system, eliminating manual entry errors.

Delivery Management

  • Route Optimization: Assign delivery tasks to drivers with optimal routing for faster delivery.
  • Order Tracking: Customers and restaurant staff can track delivery status in real time.
  • Driver Management: Monitor driver availability, performance, and delivery times.
  • Automated Notifications: Notify customers via SMS, email, or app about order confirmation, preparation, and delivery status.

Payment Integration

  • Accept payments online securely via credit/debit cards, UPI, wallets, or cash-on-delivery.
  • Automatically reconcile payments in the POS system for accurate accounting.

Analytics & Insights

  • Track online order volume, peak order times, popular dishes, and delivery performance.
  • Generate reports on revenue from online vs. in-house orders.
  • Identify areas for operational improvements or promotional campaigns.

Benefits for Restaurants

  • Expand Customer Reach: Serve customers beyond your physical location through online channels.
  • Faster Order Fulfillment: Automated order capture and optimized delivery improve speed and efficiency.
  • Reduce Errors: Eliminates mistakes caused by manual order entry.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience: Real-time tracking, notifications, and customization increase satisfaction.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Analyze ordering trends to optimize menu, pricing, and delivery logistics.

Reporting & Analytics

Sales Reporting

  • Generate daily, weekly, monthly, or custom period sales reports.
  • Track revenue by menu item, category, or outlet.
  • Identify best-selling and slow-moving items for informed menu planning.
  • Monitor discounts, promotions, and special offers to evaluate their impact on sales.

Inventory & Stock Analytics

  • Track stock usage and wastage in real-time.
  • Identify overstocked or understocked items to optimize purchasing.
  • Forecast ingredient requirements based on historical sales data.
  • Reduce losses due to expiry, spoilage, or theft.

Customer Analytics

  • Analyze customer preferences and ordering patterns.
  • Segment customers based on frequency, spend, or favorite items.
  • Measure the effectiveness of loyalty programs and promotions.
  • Gain insights for personalized marketing and improved customer engagement.

Staff Performance & Productivity Reports

  • Track employee sales, table management efficiency, and shift performance.
  • Identify high-performing staff and areas needing improvement.
  • Manage payroll and incentives based on performance metrics.

Financial & Operational Reporting

  • Monitor total revenue, profit margins, expenses, and operational costs.
  • Evaluate the performance of individual outlets (for multi-branch restaurants).
  • Track order processing times and kitchen efficiency.
  • Ensure compliance with tax regulations via automated financial reports.

Benefits of Reporting & Analytics

  • Data-Driven Decisions: Make informed choices for menu planning, staffing, and promotions.
  • Optimize Operations: Identify inefficiencies in kitchen, staff, or table management.
  • Increase Profitability: Reduce wastage, control costs, and focus on high-margin items.
  • Improve Customer Experience: Tailor offers and menu items based on customer insights.
  • Scalable Insights: Supports single outlets or multi-branch chains with consolidated reporting.

Hardware Integration

POS Terminals / Billing Stations

  • Central hub for order entry, billing, and menu management.
  • Supports touchscreen terminals for fast and intuitive operation.
  • Integrates with other devices like printers and cash drawers.

Receipt Printers

  • Print customer bills instantly at the counter or kitchen.
  • Supports thermal and dot-matrix printers for durability and speed.
  • Can generate detailed or simplified receipts and order tickets for the kitchen.

Kitchen Display Systems (KDS) / Kitchen Printers

  • Directly sends orders from POS to kitchen screens or printers.
  • Reduces order errors and ensures timely preparation.
  • Displays item modifiers, special instructions, and priority orders.

Cash Drawers

  • Securely stores cash with automatic opening linked to POS transactions.
  • Tracks cash flow and reduces manual handling errors.

Barcode & QR Code Scanners (for inventory or takeaway orders)

  • Quickly scan ingredients or packaged items for inventory tracking.
  • Supports QR code-based menu scanning for digital orders or self-service kiosks.

Card Machines / Payment Terminals

  • Accepts debit/credit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets.
  • Integrates directly with the POS for automatic transaction updates.
  • Ensures fast and secure payments for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders.

Weighing Scales / Portion Control Devices (optional)

  • Connects to POS to calculate prices for items sold by weight.
  • Ensures accurate portion pricing and reduces wastage.

Customer-Facing Displays / Kiosks

  • Shows order details and billing information in real time.
  • Enhances transparency and customer trust.
  • Can display promotional messages, offers, or loyalty points.

Benefits of Hardware Integration

  • Faster Service: Orders and payments are processed quickly, reducing wait times.
  • Error-Free Operations: Automatic communication between devices reduces mistakes.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience: Transparent billing and timely order preparation improve satisfaction.
  • Operational Efficiency: Staff can focus on service while the system handles transactions and orders.
  • Flexibility: Compatible with multiple hardware brands and types for different restaurant setups.
