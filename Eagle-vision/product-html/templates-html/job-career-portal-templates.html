Overview

Eagle Vision Technology provides dynamic, user-centric templates specifically designed for job portals and career websites. Whether you're a recruitment agency, a corporate career portal, or a job board for freelance and remote work, our templates are crafted to help you streamline hiring processes, enhance user engagement, and provide an intuitive platform for both job seekers and employers.

Ideal For

  • Recruitment Agencies
  • Corporate Career Portals
  • Freelance & Gig Work Platforms
  • Job Search Websites (General or Niche)
  • Employee Referral Platforms
  • Resume/CV Builder & Job Search Websites
  • Internship & Trainee Recruitment Portals

Key feature

Job Listings & Advanced Search

  • Search Filters: Filter job listings by industry, location, salary, job type (full-time, part-time, remote, etc.)
  • Job Categories: Multiple categories and subcategories for specialized job types
  • Search by Keywords: Allow job seekers to search by keywords, company names, or job titles
  • Sort by Relevance: Sort job listings by recent, most popular, or salary

Employer & Candidate Profiles

  • Employer Profile: Let companies create a profile with their branding, mission, and open positions
  • Job Seeker Profile: Candidates can create profiles with resumes, skills, and work experience
  • Easy Resume Upload: Simple interface to upload resumes in various formats (PDF, DOC, etc.)

Job Application & Interview Scheduling

  • One-Click Apply: Simplified application process with one-click apply buttons
  • Apply with LinkedIn: Integration with LinkedIn for easy profile import
  • Interview Scheduling: Let employers schedule interviews directly within the platform
  • Notification Alerts: Email/SMS alerts for job application status, interview invitations, and job openings

Job Alerts & Notifications

  • Customized Alerts: Job seekers can set up personalized alerts based on preferences
  • Instant Notifications: Push notifications for new job listings, application status updates, and company messages
  • Company News Updates: Employers can post news, hiring events, or company updates

Job Board & Featured Listings

  • Featured Listings: Employers can feature specific jobs for additional visibility
  • Promoted Jobs: Paid options to promote job listings to the top of the search results
  • Company Pages: Companies can list their jobs along with company details, including photos, videos, and culture information

Employer Dashboard

  • Manage Job Listings: Employers can create, edit, and track job postings
  • Review Applicants: Access resumes, cover letters, and application statuses
  • Shortlisting: Mark candidates for interviews, shortlist top applicants, and track interview progress
  • Recruitment Analytics: View metrics for views, clicks, and applications

Candidate Dashboard

  • Track Applications: Candidates can track the status of their applications (under review, shortlisted, interviewed, etc.)
  • Career Recommendations: AI-powered job suggestions based on profile, skills, and experience
  • Saved Jobs: Save favorite job listings and apply later

Job Posting & Management Tools

  • Job Post Forms: Easy-to-fill job posting form for employers
  • Job Type & Category Filters: Specify job types, experience levels, and categories when posting
  • Application Review & Management: Employers can manage applicants, send emails, and schedule follow-ups directly within the platform

Multi-Language & Multi-Currency Support

  • Global Reach: Support for international users with multi-language and multi-currency capabilities
  • Localized Settings: Ability to customize date formats, currencies, and location-based filters for different regions

SEO Optimized

  • Search Engine Optimization: Designed to help your platform rank high on search engines like Google, increasing traffic and visibility
  • Meta Tags & Structured Data: Each listing is structured to optimize for SEO, including rich snippets for better indexing

Optional Add-ons

  • Resume Builder: Let job seekers create professional resumes using a step-by-step template builder
  • Job Matching & AI Recommendations: Use AI to recommend jobs to candidates based on skills, experience, and preferences
  • Application Tracking System (ATS) Integration: Sync with leading ATS to streamline recruitment
  • Employee Referrals: Allow users to refer friends or colleagues for job openings, with rewards for successful hires
  • Job Interview Preparation: Tools and resources to help job seekers prepare for interviews (e.g., mock interviews, FAQs, etc.)
