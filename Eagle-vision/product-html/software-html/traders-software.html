Traders

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology Traders Software is a comprehensive business management solution for traders, distributors, and wholesalers, designed to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and enhance decision-making. The software offers real-time inventory tracking, fast and accurate billing, multi-location management, and customer relationship management, allowing businesses to manage purchases, sales, and stock seamlessly. With advanced reporting and analytics, traders gain actionable insights into product performance, sales trends, and profitability, while integrated POS and hardware support ensure smooth transactions. Cloud-enabled with offline functionality, it provides flexibility, security, and business continuity, helping traders save time, reduce losses, boost sales, and maintain strong client relationships—all from a single, user-friendly platform.

Inventory Management

  • Real-Time Stock Tracking: Track stock levels of multiple products across warehouses and outlets.
  • Batch & Expiry Management: Useful for perishable goods or products with limited shelf life.
  • Low Stock Alerts: Automatic notifications for reordering to avoid stockouts.
  • Multi-Warehouse Support: Manage transfers and stock distribution efficiently across locations.

Compliance & Security

Regulatory Compliance

  • Tax Compliance: Automatically calculates GST/VAT or other applicable taxes for invoices and transactions, ensuring adherence to local tax laws.

Data Security

  • User Authentication & Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Assigns permissions based on roles (e.g., admin, accountant, salesperson) to prevent unauthorized access.
