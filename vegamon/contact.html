Common Questions

What is the best way to reach Vagamon?

Vagamon is well-connected by road. The nearest airport is Cochin International Airport (100 km away) and the nearest railway station is Kottayam (75 km away). We can arrange airport transfers upon request.

Do you offer pick-up and drop services?

Yes, we provide transportation services from major arrival points including Cochin Airport, Kottayam Railway Station, and nearby cities. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to arrange transportation.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Do you have facilities for special dietary requirements?

Yes, our kitchen can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific requirements. Please inform us in advance about any dietary restrictions.
