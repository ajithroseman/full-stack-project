Overview

Eagle Vision Technology offers sleek, feature-rich real estate templates tailored for property dealers, realtors, developers, and rental platforms. Our templates help you create a modern and trustworthy online presence to list, promote, and manage properties with ease — whether it's for residential, commercial, or rental purposes.

Ideal For

  • Real Estate Agencies
  • Property Developers
  • Individual Brokers
  • Rental & Leasing Companies
  • PG/Hostel Listings
  • Commercial Building & Office Space Portals

Key feature

Elegant Property Listing

  • Grid and list layouts with thumbnails
  • Interactive image sliders & video walkthroughs
  • Badges for “Sold,” “New,” “For Rent,” etc.

Advanced Search & Filters

  • Filter by location, price, type, size, and amenities
  • Auto-suggest search bar with map integration
  • filters and tag-based search

Detailed Property Pages

  • Price, address, floor plan, amenities, and agent contact
  • Google Map embed and street view
  • Mortgage calculator and inquiry form

Agent & Agency Profiles

  • Contact details, bio, and listings by agent
  • Ratings and reviews for agents
  • Live chat and WhatsApp inquiry support (optional)

Interactive Map View

  • List properties with markers and real-time map navigation
  • Works with Google Maps or OpenStreetMap APIs

Mobile-Friendly & Responsive

  • Perfect viewing on desktop, tablets, and smartphones
  • Fast loading, optimized for all screen sizes

Admin Dashboard Templates

  • Add/Edit properties, manage inquiries and leads
  • Analytics for traffic, listings, and conversions
  • Booking history and agent performance modules

Multi-Language & Currency Support

  • Reach a global audience
  • Ready for localization and multi-currency options

Lead Generation Tools

  • Call-to-action banners, forms, and pop-ups
  • Email lead capture and CRM-ready integration

Optional Add-ons

  • Virtual tour & 3D walkthrough integrations
  • WhatsApp & Call-back buttons
  • Property comparison page
  • Blog/news section for updates and real estate advice
  • Subscription-based listing plans for multi-agent platforms
