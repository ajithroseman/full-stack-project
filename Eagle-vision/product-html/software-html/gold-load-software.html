Gold Loan Management

Gold Loan Management

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology Gold Loan Management Software is a comprehensive and secure solution designed to streamline the entire gold loan lifecycle, from valuation and disbursement to repayment and closure. It enables financial institutions to process loans quickly, accurately, and compliantly, while providing customers with flexible repayment options and timely notifications. With features like automated interest calculation, multi-channel repayment, audit-ready reporting, and regulatory compliance, the software ensures operational efficiency, minimizes errors, and enhances customer trust. By integrating real-time tracking, analytics, and reminders, Eagle Vision Technology empowers lenders to improve recovery rates, optimize loan management, and deliver a seamless borrowing experience, making it a trusted choice for banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions.

Key Features

Fast & Secure Loan Disbursement

  • Instant loan approval after gold valuation and KYC.
  • Supports multiple payout methods (cash, bank transfer, digital wallets).
  • Automated fee and interest calculations ensure accuracy.

Flexible Repayment Management

  • Multiple repayment options: EMI, bullet repayment, part-payment, or full settlement.
  • Auto interest calculation (daily, monthly, or quarterly).
  • Prepayment support with automated adjustment of interest.

Customer Communication & Engagement

  • Automated SMS/Email/WhatsApp reminders for EMIs, due dates, and overdue alerts.
  • Loan statements and receipts accessible online or via mobile.
  • Improves transparency and builds customer trust.

Regulatory Compliance & Security

  • Adheres to RBI regulations, AML, and KYC norms.
  • Digital records for audit trails and compliance checks.
  • Encrypted data storage to protect sensitive financial and customer information.

Gold Asset Management

  • Track pledged gold with weight, purity, and valuation details.
  • Secure inventory management with audit-ready records.
  • Alerts for maturity dates and auction requirements.

Advanced Reporting & Analytics

  • Real-time loan portfolio tracking (active, overdue, and closed loans).
  • Profitability and risk analysis for better decision-making.
  • Forecasting tools to monitor repayment trends and customer behavior.

Scalability & Integration

  • Suitable for NBFCs, banks, and cooperative societies.
  • Integrates with core banking, accounting, and payment gateways.
  • Cloud-ready and multi-branch support for expanding businesses.

Customer Onboarding & KYC

Gold Valuation & Authentication

Loan Eligibility & Sanctioning

Workflow Automation

  • Role-based approval process (branch officer → manager → disbursement).
  • Digital document storage with e-signature support.
  • Task automation reduces paperwork and accelerates approvals.

Risk & Compliance Checks

  • Built-in fraud detection alerts for duplicate pledges or suspicious activities.

Gold Valuation & Collateral Management

Gold Valuation

  • Standardized Valuation Process: Uses certified methods and live market rates to assess gold purity, weight, and value.
  • Purity Testing Integration: Supports karat checking through touchstone, XRF machine, or manual inspection.
  • Automated LTV (Loan-to-Value) Calculation: Calculates maximum eligible loan amount based on RBI guidelines.
  • Dynamic Rate Updates: System auto-updates with market gold rates for accurate valuation.
  • Valuation Reports: Generates detailed valuation slips, including purity, weight, and eligible loan amount.

Collateral Management

  • Secure Storage Tracking: Maintains digital records of stored gold with unique ID tagging.
  • Chain of Custody: Tracks each stage of collateral handling—from acceptance, verification, storage, to release.
  • Vault & Locker Integration: Links with vault systems to track storage locations and ensure security.
  • Insurance Integration: Allows tagging of collateral with insurance coverage details for risk management.
  • Release Management: Gold is only released after full repayment and compliance verification.

Compliance & Security

  • Regulatory Adherence: Follows RBI and AML/KYC guidelines for valuation and collateral handling.
  • Audit Trails: Every step of valuation and storage is logged for accountability.
  • Fraud Prevention: Multi-level approval for high-value loans and suspicious transactions.

Business Benefits

  • Accuracy & Fairness: Ensures customers receive transparent and fair valuation.
  • Risk Reduction: Secure collateral management prevents fraud, theft, or misplacement.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Adheres to gold loan norms, making audits smooth and hassle-free.
  • Customer Trust: Professional valuation and secure storage increase borrower confidence.
  • Operational Efficiency: Automated valuation, documentation, and collateral handling save time and reduce manual errors.

Disbursement Management

Loan Approval & Disbursement

  • Loans are disbursed immediately after verification of gold collateral, KYC documents, and loan eligibility.
  • System calculates loan amount automatically based on gold valuation, applicable LTV (Loan-to-Value) ratio, and processing fees.

Multiple Disbursement Modes

  • Supports cash, cheque, bank transfer, NEFT/RTGS, and digital wallets.
  • Flexible options for customers improve convenience and reduce processing time.

Automated Calculations

  • Interest, processing fees, and net disbursed amount are automatically calculated.
  • Ensures error-free transactions and transparency.

Regulatory Compliance & Audit Trails

  • Disbursement process follows RBI and other regulatory norms.
  • All transactions are recorded with timestamps, ensuring complete auditability.

Partial or Split Disbursement

  • Allows disbursement in installments or multiple tranches if required.
  • Useful for large loans or staged project funding.

Repayment Tracking & Reporting

Interest & Charges Calculation

Flexible Interest Calculation

  • Multiple Interest Structures: Supports flat rate, reducing balance, and simple interest methods.
  • Customizable Rates: Different interest rates can be applied based on loan amount, tenure, or customer type.
  • Daily, Monthly, or Quarterly Computation: Interest can be calculated automatically according to the agreed schedule.
  • Automatic Accrual: Interest is computed and updated in the system in real-time for accurate reporting.

Additional Charges & Fees Management

  • Processing Fees: Automatically applied at loan sanction based on predefined rules.
  • Penalty & Late Fees: Computed for overdue repayments based on customizable penalty rates.
  • Other Service Charges: Includes appraisal fees, documentation charges, or insurance premiums.
  • Transparent Breakdown: Customers and staff can see a detailed breakdown of interest and charges.

Integration with Loan Disbursement & Repayment

  • Automatically adjusts interest and fees during disbursement and repayment.
  • Prepayments or part-payments are recalculated to reflect correct interest and charges.
  • Eliminates manual errors in EMI or lump-sum payments.

Regulatory Compliance & Reporting

  • Ensures compliance with RBI guidelines on interest rates and charges.

  • Supports internal and external financial reporting requirements.
