Marketing & Database CRM

Marketing & Database CRM

Overview

Eagle Vision Technology’s Field Marketing Database CRM & Collection Management Software is designed to help businesses capture, organize, and manage customer data from field activities while ensuring smooth collection operations. It provides a centralized platform to track leads, monitor field agents, and manage customer interactions in real time, ensuring that no opportunity is missed. With smart data analytics and reporting, businesses gain valuable insights into market trends, campaign performance, and customer behavior. The integrated collection management module enables companies to automate payment tracking, set reminders, and reduce defaults, improving overall cash flow. By combining field marketing intelligence with efficient collection tools, Eagle Vision Technology empowers organizations to boost sales, improve customer engagement, and maintain financial health—making it an all-in-one solution for growing enterprises.

Key Features

Centralized Customer Database

  • Maintain a single source of truth for all customer information.
  • Store contact details, transaction history, interaction logs, and preferences.
  • Enables field agents to access up-to-date information anywhere, anytime, improving service quality.

Field Agent Management

  • Assign tasks and territories to sales or collection agents.
  • Track agent activities, visits, and follow-ups in real time.
  • Monitor performance metrics such as target achievement and conversion rates.
  • Ensures accountability and productivity across field teams.

CRM & Lead Management

  • Capture leads from multiple channels (website, calls, referrals, offline).
  • Track follow-ups, interactions, and status updates.
  • Prioritize high-value prospects and automate reminders to agents.
  • Helps increase sales conversions and ensures no opportunity is lost.

Collection Management

  • Track receivables, due dates, and payment histories.
  • Automate reminders for pending payments via SMS, email, or app notifications.
  • Generate collection reports for agents, managers, and finance teams.
  • Reduces delays in payments and improves cash flow.

Real-Time Reporting & Analytics

  • Visual dashboards for sales, collections, agent performance, and customer trends.
  • Identify areas with low performance or delayed collections.
  • Generate reports to make data-driven decisions and optimize field operations.

Mobile & Offline Functionality

  • Field agents can access customer data and log activities on mobile devices.
  • Work offline when connectivity is poor; data syncs automatically when online.
  • Ensures continuous productivity in remote or rural areas.

Integration & Scalability

  • Connects with ERP, accounting systems, and marketing tools.
  • Scales easily for growing teams, multiple territories, and expanded operations.
  • Flexible enough for small field teams or enterprise-level deployments.
